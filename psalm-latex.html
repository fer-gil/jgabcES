Psalm LaTeX / LilyPond Formatter

Example: 2,2,2,1 means lines 1–3 get two preparatory syllables, line 4 gets one. The pattern repeats for each stanza.

Use ::stanza 1, ::stanza 2, etc. Lines do not need individual numbers unless you want to override the pattern.
